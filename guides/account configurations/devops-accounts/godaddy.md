#### Account Security
- Account Settings >
    - Login & Pin >
        - [ ]  2-Step Verification > **On** (non-SMS)
        - [ ]  Active sign-ins > **Review and remove any unused or unrecognized**
    - [ ]  Delegate Access > **Review and remove any unused or unrecognized**

#### Domain Security
- [ ]  Domain Settings > DNS > DNSSEC > **On**
